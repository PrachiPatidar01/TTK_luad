# IntoGen Database Information

- *Full Database Name:* IntoGen (Integrative Onco Genomics)
- *Version Used:* IntoGen Cancer Drivers Release 2024
- *Access Date(s):* 02-11-2025
- *Persistent Identifier/URL:* https://www.intogen.org/
- *Description of Data Used:* Used to identify cancer driver genes and mutational patterns across LUAD samples by integrating somatic mutation datasets, functional impact predictions, and recurrence-based driver detection algorithms.
- *Key Parameters/Filters Applied:* LUAD-specific cohort selection, driver gene prediction modules (e.g., OncodriveFM, OncodriveCLUSTL), mutation consequence filtering, and statistical significance thresholds.
- *Licensing Information:* IntoGen resources are freely available for academic research; users should follow the usage and citation requirements outlined on the IntoGen website.
