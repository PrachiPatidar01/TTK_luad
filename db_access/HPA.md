# Human Protein Atlas Database Information

* *Full Database Name:* The Human Protein Atlas (HPA)
* *Version Used:* 2024 Release (Protein, RNA, Pathology, and Cell Atlases)
* *Access Date(s):* 01-11-2025
* *Persistent Identifier/URL:* https://www.proteinatlas.org/
* *Description of Data Used:* Retrieved protein and RNA expression profiles across tissues, cell types, and cancer samples; included immunohistochemistry-based protein localization and RNA-seq abundance to evaluate gene/protein expression patterns in LUAD.
* *Key Parameters/Filters Applied:* Cancer tissue atlas (LUAD), IHC staining intensity, RNA expression levels (FPKM/TPM), subcellular localization, and prognostic significance (when available).
* *Licensing Information:* Data are released under the Human Protein Atlas open-access license; refer to the HPA website for detailed licensing and citation requirements.
